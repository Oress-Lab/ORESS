Don't know what to do ?

Here is everything you need to know!

Welcome to the main window after logging in! You’ll see three sections: the search bar, timetable grid, and course info with favorites. Start by selecting your campus (default: Zouk Mosbeh). Ensure the correct semester and year are displayed; if not, please reach out to support.

In the search bar, type either the course code or name to find available sections. To add a course to your timetable, click its checkbox; it will appear at the correct time slot on the grid and also be added to your favorites. To favorite a course without adding it to the grid, click the star icon.

Hover over any course in the search bar to highlight its position on the timetable with a shadow effect. This action also shows detailed information about the course under Course Information, where all relevant details are provided.

Start building your schedule by selecting your courses. The favorite section syncs with the search bar, so toggling a course’s checkbox here will also update it in the search bar. The same applies to the star icon, ensuring a seamless experience.

In the icon menu above the course info section, explore features that will soon be available. The numbered icon will give you 3 possibilities for scheduling. The red PDF icon lets you download the timetable with colors and course placements. The settings icon will allow customization of course colors and time formats.

COMING SOON! Additional features are on the way, like conflict prevention for overlapping courses, highlighted by a red border and a warning message. Also, expert search symbols (e.g., @ for specific times, # for days) will help you tailor your search experience. Thank you for using ORESS!
