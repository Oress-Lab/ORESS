About ORESS

Redefining University Scheduling

Our Story

ORESS was born out of the frustration with outdated scheduling systems at NDU. As a student developer, I saw an opportunity to create a solution that's both intuitive and powerful. Our mission is to transform how students interact with their academic schedules through clean design and smart features.

The Developer

Hi, I'm Kristen W. Kamouh - full-stack developer and problem solver. With a passion for creating user-centric applications, I've poured countless hours into crafting ORESS to meet student needs. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies or contributing to open-source projects.
