The story of ORESS

ORESS is a web application I've developed to make scheduling classes at NDU a breeze. Tired of the hassle and limitations of third-party apps, I decided to take matters into my own hands. The idea for ORESS sparked during a particularly stressful registration period. As I navigated through various apps and websites, I realized the need for a streamlined and intuitive solution. I wanted to create a tool that would simplify the process, allowing students to easily visualize and manage their schedules.
